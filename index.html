Bio

Alan Aoki was born in 1940. His education in art began in 1954 at the Okyu Ito Art School in Tokyo, Japan. He went on to study art in the Roka Hasegawa Art School in Tokyo, Japan. Since then, Alan has exhibited his artwork in cities all over the world, including San Francisco, New York, Chicago, Honolulu and Tokyo.
